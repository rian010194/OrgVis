Why Choose Our Organization Chart Tool?

Built for modern organizations that need clarity, efficiency, and insight

🌳

Interactive Tree View

Navigate your organization hierarchy with an intuitive tree structure. Expand, collapse, and search through departments, teams, and individuals with ease.

🗺️

Visual Map View

See the big picture with our D3.js-powered force-directed graph. Understand relationships and connections between different parts of your organization.

📊

Metrics & Analytics

Track KPIs, budgets, headcount, and performance indicators. Visualize data with charts, graphs, and customizable dashboards.

🔗

Relationship Mapping

Define and visualize complex relationships between departments, support offices, and cross-functional teams.

🎨

Custom Branding

Personalize your organization chart with custom colors, fonts, logos, and themes that match your brand identity.

👥

Multi-Organization

Manage multiple organizations from a single interface. Perfect for consultants, agencies, or holding companies.

📱

Mobile Optimized

Access and manage your organization chart on any device. Touch-friendly interface optimized for tablets and smartphones.

🔒

Secure & Private

Your data is protected with password authentication and secure storage. Choose between local storage or cloud-based Supabase backend.

🔓

Open Source & Extensible

Built with modern web technologies and comprehensive documentation. Your IT team can easily customize, extend, and integrate with existing systems.
